The Lost Planet: An Interactive Story
Prologue
The year is 2098, and the global race for space exploration has reached a new peak. A powerful new spacefaring nation, Turkey, has constructed the "Erlik" – a starship capable of traveling to distant galaxies. As one of the six astronauts aboard the Erlik, your mission is to explore an unknown, mysterious planet in a distant galaxy. You’ve just arrived and landed on the surface of this uncharted world. Your goal is to gather information and return to Earth to report your findings.

The planet is eerie, its landscape alien and its skies unrecognizable. You’ve barely touched the surface when strange occurrences begin. Now, the survival of your team and your connection to Earth are at risk. Will you be able to uncover the truth, or will you fall victim to the planet’s dark secrets?
